About Karo Pitch

Bridging Bharat's Founders With Capital

We exist to make investor access equal — for every founder, from every city, across every sector in India.

Our Mission

Why Karo Pitch Exists

Thousands of founders across India are building real, revenue-generating businesses — but lack access to the right investors and mentorship to scale.

Karo Pitch is KaroStartup's structured pitch platform that gives these founders a direct line to investors through curated pitch events and a powerful startup discovery layer.

Think of it as a closed-room investor pitch event — made accessible to every founder, from every corner of India. Tier-2, Tier-3 cities, and Bharat entrepreneurs are our priority.
